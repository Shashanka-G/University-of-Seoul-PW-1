ABOUT

The Seoul University of Business, Tokyo is a leading school of management situated in the heart of Japan's financial centre, Tokyo. It is counted amongst the top management colleges in Japan. It functions as an autonomous institute with entrepreneurial agility and consistently ranks amongst the top ten best management institutes in world.

The Seoul University of Business offers post-graduate programmes for young Graduates, Executives, Entrepreneurs, Non-profits, Government, Returning Women and Family Businesses. These programmes offered at this institute rank among the top MBA courses in Japan.
