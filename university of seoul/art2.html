About

Department of Art has a total of 6 teaching faculty members with various specializations in Art. The Department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing equipment supported by high speed Ethernet and wireless networks.

Our faculty members aim at delivering top class education blending their rich research experience with classroom teaching. The Department has undertaken many research/consultancy projects funded both by multinationals and government agencies.

The Department has an excellent placement track record where in various companies offer salary packages. Internship opportunities are provided for final year and pre-final year students to carry out projects.

Syllabus

University of Seoul has a very extensive syllabus that is industry oriented. It is structured to maximize the learning aspects.
